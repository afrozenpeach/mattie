Hi! I'm Mattie Schraeder, a transgender game designer, blogger, and software engineer from Springfield, IL. I came out as transgender January 1st, 2017 and this blog is full of my thoughts on being transgender in the Midwest and on the Internet.

As a game designer I signed my first board game, Heckin Hounds, to be published by Sapphire City Games and released on Kickstarter in 2021. I also work on the board of directors for the board game convention, Geekway to the West.

As a software engineer I work on various projects in C#, Angular, JavaScript, and PHP. Most notably I created an editor for bibliography records called C# MARC Editor, and a Dungeons and Dragons Character Sheet Editor.
